## M01 — The Loop as Architecture

- Session: Ableton Live, 100 BPM, 4/4. Single MIDI track, pad or electric keys sound.
- Clip "M01 seed": 1-bar or 2-bar MIDI clip containing the seed cell D4–F4–G4–A4 (four 8th notes, velocities at 80).
- 1-bar version: cell starts beat 1, beats 3–4 empty.
- 2-bar version: cell in bar 1 beat 1, cell in bar 2 beat 2 (offset by one quarter note).
- Student chose one version based on feel; labeled clip "M01 seed".
- No additional tracks. The second half of the loop (empty silence) is preserved intentionally.

## M02 — Loop Craft

- Session: same Ableton session as M01, 100 BPM.
- Clip "M01 seed original": preserved, equal 8th notes D4–F4–G4–A4.
- Clip "M02 rhythm redesign": same pitches, rhythm changed to dotted 8th + 16th + 8th rest + quarter.
- Velocities: dotted 8th = 85, 16th = 65, quarter arrival = 90.
- Student ran M02 redesign for 16 repetitions; compared against original for feel.
- Carry-forward: "M02 rhythm redesign" clip is the active version for M03.

## M03 — Micro-Variation

- Session: same Ableton session as M01/M02, 100 BPM.
- Clip "A — original": M02 rhythm redesign (D4–F4–G4–A4, dotted-8th/16th rhythm), renamed.
- Clip "A′ — pitch change": same as A but G4 raised to A4. Two A4 notes now present (beat-3 and beat-4 positions).
- Clip "A″ — rhythm shift": original pitches D4–F4–G4–A4 shifted +1 16th right. Pattern starts one 16th late.
- Arrangement View layout: A × 8 bars → A′ × 8 → A × 4 → A″ × 8 → A × 4 = 32 bars total.
- Carry-forward: all three clips A, A′, A″ available for further layering.

## M04 — The Pulse as Foundation

- Session: same Ableton session as M01–M03, 100 BPM.
- New track: MIDI percussion track (Korg Volca Beats, hi-hat sample, or similar single-pitch instrument).
- Clip "M04 pulse": 1-bar MIDI clip, 16 16th-note steps at velocities: 100·50·70·45 | 90·50·70·45 | 100·50·70·45 | 85·50·70·45.
- Percussion track volume balanced so melody (M03 clips A, A′, A″) remains primary.
- Test: muting percussion still reveals implied beat from melodic rhythm.
- Carry-forward: M03 melody arrangement + M04 pulse track both active and looping.

## M05 — Additive & Subtractive Process

- Session: same Ableton session as M01–M04, 100 BPM.
- New MIDI track with 4 additive clips: M05-A1 [D], M05-A2 [D F], M05-A3 [D F G], M05-A4 [D F G A].
- Each clip is 1 bar; notes placed at beats 1, 3, 2, 4 respectively (structural-first ordering).
- Arrangement View: A1×4 → A2×4 → A3×4 → A4×4 → A3×4 → A2×4 → A1×4 → 4 bars silence = 32 bars total.
- M04 pulse track runs throughout as rhythmic constant.
- Carry-forward: 4 additive clips (M05-A1 through M05-A4) in a 32-bar arc; ready for phase duplication in M06.

## M06 — Phase Shifting

- Session: same Ableton session as M01–M05, 100 BPM.
- New track: Phase B — duplicate of M05-A4 (full D–F–G–A pattern), nudged forward by student's chosen offset (1–4 16th notes).
- Phase B runs simultaneously with M05-A4 in Session View; student chose the offset that sounded most interesting.
- M04 pulse track continues throughout as rhythmic constant.
- Carry-forward: M05 additive arc + Phase B at chosen offset + M04 pulse = three active layers.

## M07 — Rhythmic Displacement

- Session: same Ableton session as M01–M06, 100 BPM.
- Four displacement clips created from the main melody clip: Disp-0 (no shift), Disp-1 (+1 8th), Disp-2 (+2 8ths), Disp-3 (+3 8ths).
- Arrangement View layout: Disp-0 × 4 bars → Disp-1 × 4 bars → Disp-2 × 4 bars → Disp-3 × 4 bars = 16 bars total.
- Phase B runs across all 16 bars; M04 pulse track runs throughout.
- Carry-forward: 4 displacement clips in a 16-bar sequence, layered against Phase B and pulse.

## M08 — Polyrhythm & Cross-Rhythm

- Session: same Ableton session as M01–M07, 100 BPM.
- New MIDI track (spare timbre: pluck, bell, or muted key).
- Clip "M08 poly": 1-bar MIDI clip, three quarter notes — D4 (beat 1), A4 (beat 2), F4 (beat 3), beat 4 empty. 3-beat pattern in 4/4.
- Clip loops in Session View alongside Disp-0 through Disp-3, Phase B, and M04 pulse.
- Coincidence point (D4 + melody both on beat 1) occurs every 4 bars; locator "POLY ANCHOR" placed in Arrangement View.
- Carry-forward: 3 active layers (melody/displacement arc, Phase B, M08 poly) + pulse. POLY ANCHOR marks structural reset for M15.
